Here at the Grange Medical Centre we have an expert clinic with an amazing team of General Practitioners and Medical Healthcare Experts who are among the first clinics in Ireland to use the HealthTrak System in Ireland.

Dr Joan Hurley

Senior Family Practitioner

Having founded the Grange Medical Centre over twenty years ago to serve the Ennis and surrounding communities in County Clare, I believe using HealthTrak will help us to move forward into a new and exciting era in Family Practice Medicine. I believe it will be a fantastic tool for our clinic!
